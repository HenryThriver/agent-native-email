# Support

Agent-Native Email is an experimental, open-source reference build. Support is
best effort, not a managed service or uptime commitment.

For help, email `henry@thrivinghenry.com` with a subject beginning
`[Agent-Native Email]` and include:

- the exact guide version;
- fresh setup or migration;
- the phase and last completed receipt;
- the command that failed and a redacted error;
- what you expected to happen.

Never email passwords, API tokens, private keys, recovery codes, full mailbox
exports, or private message content. Revoke and rotate any secret that was
accidentally exposed before asking for help.

The fastest support request is a reproducible problem against the public
starter. Provider outages, account verification, billing decisions, domain
purchases, and recovery of infrastructure modified outside the guide remain
the operator's responsibility.
